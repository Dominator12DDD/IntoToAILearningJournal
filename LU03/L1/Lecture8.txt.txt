Unit 3 Lecture 1 - Multi-Media

Multi-Media - Any format of media (Videos, Photos, Audio)

AI Generated Multi-Media - Media generated by AI, media altered by AI, video games generated by AI

Text-to-Speech (TTS) - using a piece of text and having it said by an AI

Voice Cloning - using audio sample to make cloned voice

Text to video - using a written prompt to create a video generated by AI

Image-to-video - using a image to create an AI generated video

AI art tends to be more yellowish, AI believes that golden color makes images look best
