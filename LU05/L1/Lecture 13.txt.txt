Unit 5 Lecture 1 - Hardware

What hardware is required to run AI?

Hardware - Machinery that runs the computer.

Motherboard - circuit board, connects different components. Most important component is the CPU.

CPU - Does all the thinking and computing. Everything that thinks in the computer connects to the CPU "We as apes made sand think" Luke Matheis 2025

Power Supply - converts the power, turns energy into heat

RAM - Random Access Memory, volatile, turning computer off an on clears RAM

Storage - stores data (Hard disk drive, solid state drive)

Expansion drives - plug ins

GPU - Graphic Processing Unit, does everything the CPU doesn't want to do

Raspberry Pi - mini computer
