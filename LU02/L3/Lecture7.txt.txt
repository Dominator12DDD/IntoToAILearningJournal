Unit 2 Lecture 3 - Chain of Thought

Chain of Thought - Asking an AI how it got that answer. It basically cheats/lies about how it got that answer, but can help next prompt be more accurate next time.

Chain of Density - reducing text you want summarized. You keep reducing size of text till you have a concise summary. Boiling down a large piece of text to a small summary.

RAG - going beyond the vanilla limitations of generative AI (LLM)

RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation
